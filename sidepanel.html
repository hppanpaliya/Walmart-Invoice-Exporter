Help & FAQ

How to Use the Extension

Single Order Download:

  1. Navigate to a specific Walmart order page
  2. Click the extension icon — the order appears automatically
  3. Check its box, then click "Single file"

Batch Download:

  1. Go to your Walmart order history page (walmart.com/orders)
  2. Click the extension icon
  3. Click "Load my orders" (first time) or "Check for new orders" (after that) — page limit and "only new" are under Options
  4. Wait for the order numbers to load
  5. Select the orders you want to download
  6. Click "Single file" (one workbook) or "Multiple files" (one per order)
Note: Do not close the panel while downloading. The side panel can stay open while you browse other tabs.
Required Chrome Settings for Bulk Downloads

1. Configure Download Settings:

  1. Open Chrome settings: chrome://settings/downloads
  2. Turn OFF "Ask where to save each file before downloading"
  3. Turn OFF "Show downloads when they're done"

2. Enable Automatic Downloads:

  1. Open: chrome://settings/content/automaticDownloads
  2. Under "Allowed to automatically download multiple files", click Add
  3. Enter "[*.]walmart.com" and click Add
Tip: Click the links above to copy them, then paste into a new tab.
Download Issues & Tips

If you're experiencing download issues:

  • Slow internet - try downloading fewer orders at once
  • Order age - very old orders might take longer
  • Browser resources - try closing other tabs
Recommendation: For better reliability, download 5-10 orders at a time.
Saved orders & your data

Every order you download is saved on this device (in the browser's IndexedDB) — nothing expires and nothing is ever sent anywhere:

  • Saved automatically: Once an order is downloaded, its full details are stored locally
  • Instant re-export: Re-downloading a saved order (in any format) reuses the stored data instead of reopening the order page — and nothing is ever fabricated
  • Faster re-syncs: Because your orders are remembered, "Check for new orders" can skip ones it already knows — turn on "only new orders" under Options
  • Yours to delete: Settings → "Data on this device" → "Delete all saved data" wipes everything the extension has stored, in one step
Note: There's no automatic expiration and no separate "cache" to manage — one control clears everything.
