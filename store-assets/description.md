## Summary (132-char field)

Export your Walmart order history to Excel, CSV, or PDF — and see where the money went on a built-in spending dashboard.

## Detailed description

See your entire Walmart spending on a private dashboard — and export every order to Excel in two clicks — for tax season, expense reports, budgeting, or just knowing where the money went.

Walmart Invoice Exporter collects every order from walmart.com/orders, saves it privately on your device, and turns it into clean, ready-to-use files. The built-in Spending Dashboard shows monthly totals, complete invoices you can expand right in the table, price changes on things you rebuy, and your most-bought items — all from your own data, all on-device.

★ SPENDING DASHBOARD
• Click any order to expand its full invoice inline — items, prices, the complete money breakdown, and payment methods
• Interactive monthly chart: click a bar to scope the whole page to that month, then export just that month
• Stat cards with vs-previous deltas, plus insights: biggest order, savings rate, average items per order, busiest day, most-bought item
• Price watch: see when items you rebuy get more expensive
• Search and sort every order, grouped by month with select-the-whole-month checkboxes
• The live side panel sits right beside the dashboard — collect, fetch, and export without leaving the page

★ EXPORT ANYTHING, ANY WAY
• Excel (.xlsx) — one combined workbook or one file per order, with optional product photos
• CSV — spreadsheet-friendly, with QuickBooks and Xero bank-import presets
• JSON — for scripts, automations, and AI tools
• PDF & printable receipts — clean documents with item lists and barcodes
• Or "Save details to library (no file)" — store invoices locally and export any time, instantly

★ FAST, FLEXIBLE COLLECTION
• "Load my orders" collects your full history; "Check for new orders" tops it up in seconds
• Optional fast mode reuses your own logged-in session instead of clicking through pages — with automatic fallback to classic paging
• Collect with Walmart's own filters: order type (online, in-store, in-progress, completed, returned) and a date range
• Multiple Walmart accounts stay cleanly separated (detected by a privacy-preserving hash — never your name)

★ PRIVATE BY DESIGN
• No accounts, no servers, no tracking, no telemetry
• Your orders never leave your device — everything is processed locally
• Optional housekeeping: saved data is wiped only after 180 days of not using the extension (adjust or turn off)
• One honest "Delete all saved data" button — plus per-account delete
• Open source on GitHub for full transparency
• Optional AI access (off by default): let Claude or any MCP tool on your own computer read your saved orders via the walmart-invoice-mcp helper (on npm) — read-only, local-only, token-protected

★ PERFECT FOR
• Tax reporting and reconciliation
• Business expense tracking
• Family budgeting and spending analysis
• Feeding your purchase history into spreadsheets or AI tools

★ GETTING STARTED
1. Open walmart.com/orders and click the extension icon
2. Click "Load my orders" (or "Check for new orders") and let it collect
3. Select orders and download — or click "View dashboard" to see where the money went

Bulk downloads work best with Chrome's "Ask where to save each file" setting off — the in-app Help & FAQ walks you through it.

★ WHAT'S NEW IN 8.1
• Full-app dashboard: expandable inline invoices, interactive monthly chart, and a new insights card
• Fast collection with "only new orders" support and automatic classic fallback
• Walmart's own order filters (type + date range) as collection options
• Multi-account support with per-account data and delete
• Optional AI access (MCP): ask Claude about your spending — off by default, read-only, never leaves your device
• Complete visual redesign — light and dark — across panel, settings, and dashboard

Note: This extension works only on walmart.com (and, optionally, walmart.ca).
