## Summary (132-char field)

Export your Walmart order history to Excel, CSV, or PDF — and see where the money went on a built-in spending dashboard.

## Detailed description

Export your entire Walmart order history to Excel in two clicks — for tax season, expense reports, budgeting, or just knowing where the money went.

Walmart Invoice Exporter collects every order from walmart.com/orders, saves it privately on your device, and turns it into clean, ready-to-use files. Then the built-in Spending Dashboard shows you totals by month, price changes on things you rebuy, and your most-bought items — all from your own data, all offline.

★ EXPORT ANYTHING, ANY WAY
• Excel (.xlsx) — one combined workbook or one file per order, with optional product photos
• CSV — spreadsheet-friendly, ready for Google Sheets or accounting tools
• JSON — for scripts, automations, and AI tools
• PDF & printable receipts — clean documents with item lists and barcodes
• Works with Walmart's own filters (date range, status, search) so you export exactly what you need

★ SPENDING DASHBOARD
• Total spent, orders, monthly average, and savings at a glance
• Click any month to rescope the whole page — then export just that month
• Price watch: see when items you rebuy get more expensive
• Most bought: what you actually buy, by count and total cost
• Search every order and item you've ever bought

★ FAST, RELIABLE COLLECTION
• One click collects your full order history — set it and forget it
• "Only new orders" mode tops up your history in seconds
• Orders are saved locally, so repeat exports are instant — no re-crawling

★ PRIVATE BY DESIGN
• No accounts, no servers, no tracking, no telemetry
• Your orders never leave your device — everything is processed locally
• One honest "Delete all saved data" button removes everything instantly
• Open source on GitHub for full transparency

★ PERFECT FOR
• Tax reporting and reconciliation
• Business expense tracking
• Family budgeting and spending analysis
• Feeding your purchase history into spreadsheets or AI tools

★ GETTING STARTED
1. Open walmart.com/orders and click the extension icon
2. Click "Check for new orders" and let it collect
3. Select orders and download — or open the Spending Dashboard

Bulk downloads work best with Chrome's "Ask where to save each file" setting off — the in-app Help & FAQ walks you through it.

★ WHAT'S NEW IN 7.3
• All-new Spending Dashboard — a full page with monthly charts, price watch, and drill-down export
• New export formats: CSV, JSON, PDF, and printable receipts alongside Excel
• Dark mode, redesigned order list, and a Settings page
• Orders now persist locally until you delete them — repeat exports are instant

Note: This extension works only on walmart.com.
